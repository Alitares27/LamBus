LamBus

Quality in Motion

Purpose

Why does my business exist?

This business exists due to the need in the city for an automated system that helps people save time and money when they want to take a bus or taxi service.

What mission is my business attempting to accomplish?

Help society improve their lifestyle by providing a safe and quality service that saves time and money.

Approach

How is my business different?

This will be the first platform to integrate taxi and bus services, and it would also be the only tool to view city traffic, routes and schedules in real time.

What makes my service or product better or unique?

To be the only platform that provides people with information and bus and taxi services supported by technology that saves time and money for all users at the same time.

Values

What words or phrases best describe my business and its culture?

  • Improve the quality of life by applying technology daily.
  • Integration in motion
